I have warm and fuzzy feelings about Cult of the Lamb. I came to the game entirely by accident in December of last year, first listening to the soundtrack because my sister said it reminded her of me. I was really touched by that. I was going through a tough time, it being winter, and so I added a few tracks from the OST to keep my spirits up. Turns out Ray's brother had gifted them a copy for their birthday. We share Steam libraries, so I got to benefit from that. I first launched the game on Christmas Day of 2023 and the rest is history!

I don't even play video games that often... I think my obsession with the silly lamb game stems from the fact that this is the type of thing my 12-year-old furry self would have been all over. It's like emotional junk food for me.
